Leah Binnall

Fashion Stylist

Leah Binnall is a London based fashion stylist working with an eclectic mix of clients and celebrities across commercials, television, film and editorial campaigns.

Having started her career as an apprentice on Saville Row, Leah has an in-depth knowledge of garment construction, from the quality of the cloth, to the cut of the design.

Further experience of working alongside Giles Deacon as a pattern cutter has resulted in a keen eye for tailoring, with a strong passion for contemporary menswear.
